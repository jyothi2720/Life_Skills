* I feel energetic and motivated

* My body feels active, not tired

* Ideas come easily

* I enjoy what I’m doing

## Question 5: What are some ideas that you can implement to sleep better?

* Sleep at the same time every day

* Avoid mobile/laptop before sleep

* Keep the room dark and cool

* Avoid heavy food before sleeping

* Reduce caffeine (coffee/tea) at night

* Relax and calm your mind before sleeping

## Question 7: What are some steps you can take to exercise more?

* Start with small exercises (5–10 minutes)

* Choose an activity you enjoy (walking, dancing, cycling)

* Set a simple daily routine

* Walk instead of using vehicles for short distances

* Do stretching or yoga at home

* Use reminders or alarms

* Take stairs instead of lifts

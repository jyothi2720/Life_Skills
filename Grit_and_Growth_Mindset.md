
# Grit and Growth Mindset

## Question 1 : Grit : Paraphrase (summarize) the video in a few (1 or 2) lines. Use your own words.
* Grit means not giving up and keeping on trying even when things are hard.

* People succeed more because they keep working, not because they are born talented.

## Question 2 : Growth Mindset: Paraphrase (summarize) the video in a few (1 or 2) lines in your own words.

* A growth mindset means believing you can improve by learning, practicing, and trying new strategies.

* Your abilities grow when you accept mistakes and keep working instead of giving up.

## Question 3: What is the Internal Locus of Control? What is the key point in the video?

### What is Internal Locus of Control?

* It means believing you are responsible for your actions, results, and success—not luck or external things.

* You feel in control of your choices and outcomes.

### Key point in the video:

* You stay motivated when you understand that you create your future through your decisions, effort, and attitude.

## Question 5: What are your ideas to take action and build Growth Mindset?

**Simple ideas you can follow:**

* Practice daily even when it's difficult

* Set small learning goals every week

* Don’t fear mistakes—treat them as lessons

* Keep improving your coding step by step

* Try new problems instead of repeating easy ones

* Surround yourself with positive, learning-focused people

* Stay consistent even on low-motivation days

The Feynman Technique is a method where you learn something by explaining it in simple words as if teaching someone else.

## 3. What are active and diffused modes of thinking?

* **Active mode:** When you focus hard on a problem and use deep concentration.

* **Diffused mode:** When your mind relaxes and ideas flow freely, helping you make new connections.

## 4. According to the video, what are the steps to take when approaching a new topic?

* Choose a skill you really care about.

* Break the skill into smaller parts.

* Remove distractions.

* Learn just enough to start practicing.


## 5. What are some of the actions you can take going forward to improve your learning process?

* Study with full focus and avoid multitasking.

* Keep your phone silent and remove unnecessary notifications.

* Block social media during study time.

* Track your time to know how long you actually work.

* Set deadlines to manage time but focus on understanding, not rushing.

* Try to explain concepts in your own words.

* Practice regularly so you can remember and apply what you learn.

* Communicate early when you face issues or need help.
